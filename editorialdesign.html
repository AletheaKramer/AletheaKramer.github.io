Editorial Design

Concept design of magazine and editorial spread

Concept and visual style of a magazine was developed and designed. I excersized my skills in typesetting and layout to create simple, effective, and readable spreads. Description: Repose is for the mindful urbanite; those who are as likely to mediate as they are to crush deadlines. We believe that the biggest gift one can give themself is time, pause, and slowness. Our magazine offers opportunities for slow; ways that busy professionals can balance their days and ﬁnd wellbeing. Each quarter we feature ways to create, consume, move, eat, and think with slowness at its core. Slow lifestyle isn’t about giving up modern life, but ﬁnding ways to stop the clock for a while.
