Cadence Cycle & Movement Studio Brand

Complete Brand Design and Development

Complete brand package for Vancouver cycle and movement studio. In consultation with the studio owner, I developed all visual assets from ideation to production. Assets included logo, work mark, colours, typography, photo treatment, custom patterns, and merchandise design. I prepared all files into usable package for client to access and use independently. I also prepared all print files.
